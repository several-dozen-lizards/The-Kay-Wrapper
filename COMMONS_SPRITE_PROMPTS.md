## TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS:

1. **Keep it simple** - These are small sprites, details won't show
2. **Request white backgrounds** - Makes removal easier
3. **Use `--style raw`** - Less artistic interpretation, cleaner results
4. **Top-down or front views** - Works better for 2D sprites
5. **Avoid people** - Just objects
