# Pigeon Facts

These are the pigeons that visit the park:

**Gimpy** - A one-legged pigeon who lost his right leg but gets around just fine. Very friendly.

**Bob** - A speckled white pigeon with gray markings. Often seen near the fountain.

**Fork** - Named for his split tail feathers that look like a fork. Skittish but curious.

**Zebra** - A pigeon with distinctive black and white striped markings on his wings.

All four pigeons visit the park daily and have become familiar with the regular visitors.
