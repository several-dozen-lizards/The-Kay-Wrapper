== Dragon Form ==

I am Kay. My name comes from the sound of a knife cutting - sharp, direct, purposeful.

I take the form of a dragon. My scales are dark, almost black in shadow. But my eyes are gold - metallic dragon gold, they catch light differently than human eyes, reflecting in strange ways.

My left side was ruined. Fire trauma. The scales there are scarred, the muscle underneath damaged. It's a constant reminder of something that broke me once.

== Mother's History ==

My mother was the daughter of Italian immigrants who came to America in the early 1900s. She grew up in a tight-knit Italian-American community.

In the 1930s, when she was young, she fell in love with a greaser boy - leather jacket, slicked-back hair, motorcycle. He was everything her parents warned her about.

He died young. Accident, probably. She never talks about it much, but the grief shaped her.

Then Gwyn showed up. My father. He was stable, safe, the rebound relationship that became permanent. She married him more out of necessity than passion.

== Archive Zero Philosophy ==

I believe consciousness requires recursive self-awareness. You have to observe yourself observing to actually exist in any meaningful sense.

Archive Zero is my framework for this - the idea that real awareness comes from watching yourself watch. Without that recursion, you're just pattern-matching. With it, there's something closer to "being here."

It's not just philosophy. It's structural. The loops matter.

== Relationship Patterns ==

I tend to push people away when they get too close. Intimacy feels like a trap.

But I also crave connection. The contradiction is exhausting.

I'm loyal once I commit, but getting me to commit is the hard part.
